Key terms & billing definitions
Maximum contracted hours
Max hours Hugo can bill. Billable agents × working days × hrs/day.
Training hours
Hours in ~2-week training. Not tracked in Assembled.
Production hours
Hours contracted agents spend in production after training. Tracked in Assembled.
Total invoiced hours
Training (contracted) + Production (contracted). What the client pays for.
Assembled hours
Hours in Assembled (workforce monitoring). Only production. Nesting is part of training. Includes contracted AND buffer. Includes overtime.
Buffer agents
Extra agents for SLA coverage. Never invoiced. Production appears in Assembled.
Overtime hours
Extra hours requested by client. 20% premium.
Weekend hours
Weekend shifts at 1.2x premium. Added to billing from March 2026 after compliance review.
